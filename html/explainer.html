About this Collection

This page explains what the various stats for each route in my collection represent.

Road Cycling (route is suitable for all bikes)

Gravel Cycling (may not be suitable for Road bikes)

Cross Country Mountain Biking (not suitable for bikes without Suspension)

Downhill Mountain Biking (not suitable for bikes without Full Suspension)

Effort Level

This number is based on a scale of 1 to 5.

  • 1 represents very minimal physical exertion (i.e. you should not get out of breath).
  • 2 represents minimal physical exertion (i.e. occasionally you could get out of breath).
  • 3 represents aerobic physical exertion (i.e. you should get out of breath but still be able to hold short conversations).
  • 4 represents high physical exertion (i.e. you should get out of breath and struggle to hold short conversations).
  • 5 represents very high physical exertion. You should not attempt this unless you are in a trained state without any illnesses/injuries.

Adrenaline Rating

This is a subjective number based on a scale of 1 to 5. Where 1 represents a route that is not intended to be scary and 5 represents something only for the thrillseekers (i.e. expect high speeds and regular "Oh Shit!" moments )
